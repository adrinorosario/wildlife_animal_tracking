Wildlife helpline and conservation platform

Features:

    •	AI recognition of injured/lost animals- Rangers, tourists, locals/anybody who encounters an injured/lost animal in the woods/urban areas can click a picture of them and send an alert to the nearby vet/officers who can send help.
    •	Emergency SOS- Activate a siren like broadcast to all nearby clinics, rangers, officers for quicker help.
    •	GPS tracking- The user selects the current location of where they are reporting the incident from. Maps used to signal the others
    •	Offline maps, alerts, SOS features
    •	Categories of alert:
    •	 Injury: 2 days timeframe- RED
    •	Sighting: 6 hours- BLUE
    •	Lost in urban areas: 1 day- YELLOW
    •	Displaced or isolated: 12 hours- ORANGE- emits radius

Users:
• Tourists, Locals, Urban public
• Rangers, Doctors, vets, Forest officers

Functionality:
• Automatic location mapping: When you launch the app, it traces your current location and filters out the data required by the AI model.
• Camera: Take picture, model recognises the location, species etc. and sends the alert. If online, image gets uploaded immediately. If offline, only the credentials are broadcasted and the image is uploaded once online.
• Alert pin: Will contain name, location, type of emergency and will redirect to google map
• Catalog with all pins uploaded with status, date, type, images etc- available online: This page appears after reporting is done, this is not the primary home page.
• Map: The pins of the current location will be highlighted when zoomed in. As we zoom out, only the most spotted animals will appear.
• AI model: Recognises the animal, fills out needed information in the details page.

Pages:
• Map with pins, recently updated.
• Camera page that slides/ pops up
• Details page with categories etc and then submit- map gets updated
• Back to default page-map
• Emergency notifications
• Catalog
• Settings
• Admin Login in settings:
• Ranger credentials-login
• Emergency siren only for admin- extreme injury case
• Acknowledgment of pin-only for admin: Rescued/Treated
